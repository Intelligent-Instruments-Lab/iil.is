# Thor Magnusson presenting at the SHIFT Festival

The Royal Ballet and Opera in London is running the [SHIFT festival](https://www.rbo.org.uk/tickets-and-events/festival/rboshift-2026-details) that explores the shifting boundaries between opera and technology. The focus this year is AI. Thor Magnusson will present work of the Intelligent Instruments lab and join a panel with Rebecca Fiebrink and Netia Jones.

The programme of the day is [here](https://www.rbo.org.uk/tickets-and-events/shift-exchange-details)
