### Decentralised Creativity and Agential Systems in Music

### Web Audio Conference 2025

The international [Web Audio Conference](https://wac-2025.ircam.fr/) will be held at [Ircam](https://www.ircam.fr/) this year. For this 10th year anniversary, the proposed theme "Hacking and Making with Web Audio", is intended to invite scholars, researchers, developers, designers and artists to explore and engage with new usage of the Web Audio API across various disciplines and contexts

Thor Magnusson will give a keynote during the conference called "Thinking with Technology: Hacking, Making and Improvisation"

Time and place: Ircam, Paris. November 19th, 2025.
