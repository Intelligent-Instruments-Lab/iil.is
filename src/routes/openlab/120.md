## Vegetal Media.    

For this Open Lab 120, PhD researcher Thomas Pausz will present recent experiments under the topic Vegetal Media, including experimental vibratory interfaces inspired by buzz-pollinating insects and the current design of a library of sensors for greenhouse ecosystem exploration. 
The session will be followed by a participatory pub quiz on the history of plant technological mediation.   

**Bio:**  
Thomas Pausz is a speculative designer and doctoral researcher at the Intelligent Instruments Lab, and a professor at the Iceland University of the Arts. His interests span more-than-human ecologies, interspecies media, and ecofiction. In his doctoral project, he designs and tests multimodal dispositifs for co-sensing and co-creating with non-human agencies, while exploring the philosophy and ethics of our mediated relations with the environment through the lens of vegetal life.
