**Name:** Seancoil
**Type:** Electromagnetic actuator
**Principle:**  When charged the coil creates a magnetic field.
**Function**: The frequency of the AC power supplied to the coil will translate to the created magnetic field which in turn vibrates a magnet you place in the pit of the coil (or some ferromagnetic material).
**Voltage:** AC from Audio Amplifier
**Typical use:** Attach a magnet to something, use the coil for vibrating that magnet (and whatever it is attached to).
**Good for:** Light-touch excitation of an acoustic element you want to resonate with an audio signal.
**Bad for:** High fidelity audio reproduction (being a straight up speaker). Also, it will not introduce a huge amount of power to a dense object, a light stiff surface will be easier to move (drum skin, styrofoam, perhaps acoustic guitar top).
