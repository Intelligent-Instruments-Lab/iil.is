# Mini Jack Cable

Type: Connector
Added: January 9, 2023 3:47 PM
Senses: Control Voltage (CV), Supply Power
Actuates: Connects our elements to one another
Status: Done
Panel: Processing Panel - Utilities Box

We have various lengths of these cables to connect all the elements in Organolib.

![A stereo Mini Jack cable.](mini-jack-cable-1.jpg)

A stereo Mini Jack cable.

You need a little awareness of how you are connecting elements. For example you may not want to connect a power supply into a sensor input, in other words these cables will connect to most of our elements but gain an understanding of the connections you want to make before you plug in.

They come in (mostly) two flavours, Mono and Stereo. The Mono provides two strands of connection and Stereo provides three:
