**Name:** IANG
**Type:** Wireless controller for each hand.
**Principle:** Accelerometer and pushbuttons
**Function:** Detects relative motion of the controller and has mappable buttons
**Voltage: 3V
Typical use:** As a rugged, stable controller for your software.
**Good for:** Hands free control of your software.
**Bad for:** Extremely low latency uses (< 10 ms)
