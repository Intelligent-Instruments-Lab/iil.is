# Goosenecks

Type: Connector
Added: January 16, 2023 1:51 PM
Senses: Allows you to adjust the neck shape.
Actuates: And allows you to arrange your Organium elements in space.
Status: Done

![imageedit_73_2984960355-removebg-preview.jpg](goosenecks-3.jpg)

Quick mounting and positioning of sensors, microphones, cameras, lights, and other Organium elements. Arranging components spatially for performance setups, installations, or prototyping.

**3D Printed Addon:**

- 4mm hole diameter for M4 screws
- Compatible with standard Organolib M4 mounting points
- STL files available in lab repository

![IMG_20230116_135206.jpg](goosenecks-1.jpg)

We have made our own preliminary attachment addon which gives you a 4mm hole which comfortably accept M4 mechanical screws. These holes are found on many Organium elements for attaching them to one another and other stuff
